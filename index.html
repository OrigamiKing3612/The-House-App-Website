About the House System

Originating in British Commonwealth boarding schools, the house system has evolved into a cornerstone of educational institutions worldwide. It not only provides organizational structure but also cultivates leadership and community among students.

About The App

The House App is a cutting-edge tool designed to enrich the educational journey for students and teachers alike. It offers insights to track house-related activities and achievements, fostering engagement within the school community.
