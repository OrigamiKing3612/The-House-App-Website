You can install The House App as an app on your device for quick access and extra features. Follow the instructions below based on your device type

## iOS / iPadOS

- Tap the **Share** button (square with an arrow pointing up).
- Scroll down and select **Add to Home Screen**.
- Tap **Add**.


## macOS Safari

- In Safari: Click **File &gt; Add to Dock**
- This will create a shortcut in your Dock.


## Chrome, Edge, Firefox

- Open The House App in your browser.
- Look for the **Install** icon in the address bar (usually a + or a computer icon).
- Click it and confirm installation to create a desktop or start menu shortcut.

### If you can not find it

- Press the three dots in the top right corner
- Go down to **Cast, save, and share**
- Select **Install page as app...**


## Android

- Open The House App in Chrome.
- Tap the **Menu** button (three dots in the upper-right corner).
- Select **Add to Home screen**.
- Choose a name for the app and tap **Add**.
